## Sample Evaluation 1

### Prompt
"What are the benefits of remote work?"

### AI Response A
Remote work allows employees to work from home and save travel time. It can make people happier.

### AI Response B
Remote work offers flexibility, reduces commuting time, improves work-life balance, and expands access to global talent for companies.

### Evaluation
Response B is stronger due to:
- Specific benefits
- Clear structure
- Broader perspective
- More informative content

### Improved Final Answer
Remote work offers flexibility, reduces commute time, improves work-life balance, and allows companies to hire talent globally. When implemented well, it can increase productivity and employee satisfaction.

## Sample Evaluation 2

### Prompt
"Explain two-factor authentication in simple terms."

### AI Response
Two-factor authentication is when you use two things to log in.

### Issues Identified
- Too vague
- No examples
- Low usefulness

### Improved Answer
Two-factor authentication adds an extra security step when logging in. Along with your password, you verify your identity using something like a code sent to your phone or an authentication app, making accounts much harder to hack.
